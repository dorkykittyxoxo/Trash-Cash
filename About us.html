FAQs

Q: How does Trash Cash work? A: We help individuals and businesses recycle waste responsibly. You can schedule a pickup or drop off your recyclables at our designated centers.

Q: What types of waste do you accept? A: We accept paper, plastic, metals, e-waste, and more. Check our waste category list for details.

Q: Can I earn rewards for recycling? A: Yes! Our reward system allows you to earn points for recycling, which can be redeemed for cash or discounts with our partners.

Q: How can businesses collaborate with Trash Cash? A: We offer corporate waste management solutions. Contact us to set up a customized recycling plan for your business.
